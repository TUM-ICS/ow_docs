Computes the numeric integration of an AngularVelocity omega(t)

Integrates AngularVelocity to AngularPosition over the time interval dt by assuming a constant velocity over dt.

q_[n+1] = Exp(w)*q_[n] Exp(w) = [cos(||w||*dt/2), sin(||w||*dt/2) * w / ||w||]

The orientation Q[n+1] is expressed in the fixed frame base of Q_b. Q[n+1]_b = quaternionIntegration(Q[n+1]_b, Q[n]_b, omega_b)

First derivative of SLERP.

First Order Derivate of interpolation between intial and final AngularPosition wrt to parameterization s.

Note
This is the derivative of the quaternion coefficients, not the time derivative (angular velocities).

QsP = d slerp(Qi,Qf,s) / ds

See: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slerp
