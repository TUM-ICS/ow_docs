Detailed Description

The FootZmpEstimator class.

This class calculates the center of pressure of one foot sole from ankle FT sensor wrench wrt Sole frame.

The standard method to compute the ZMP is based directly on the FT sensor information of each foot [Kajita et al. 2014].
